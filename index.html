Ship Classification

Cargo
A ship designed for transporting goods and general cargo.
Example: Container ship, Bulk carrier
Carrier
A ship used for transporting aircraft, typically for military purposes.
Example: Aircraft carrier
Cruise
A ship built for leisure travel and passenger transportation.
Example: Cruise ship
Military
A ship operated by the armed forces for various military purposes.
Example: Destroyer, Frigate
Tankers
Ships designed for transporting liquids, such as oil, gas, or chemicals, in bulk.
Example: Oil tanker, LNG carrier

About

Ship Classification is a project aimed at classifying ships into five different types: Cargo, Carrier, Cruise, Military, and Tankers. The classification is based on various features and characteristics of the ships, such as their design, purpose, and cargo capacity.

The project utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms and techniques to analyze ship data and make accurate predictions regarding their classification. The trained model takes into account various parameters and patterns to classify an input ship image into one of the five types.

Ship Classification offers a user-friendly interface where users can upload their ship images and receive instant classification results. The model has been trained on a diverse dataset of ship images, ensuring reliable and accurate classification outcomes.
